Crispy Sesame Tofu!

Description

You've never had Tofu Scramble like this before! It has a rich and creamy texture and surprisingly eggy flavor that'll remind you of real scrambled eggs! Plus, it’s quick and easy to make and so versatile to use. The perfect vegan breakfast that'll change your mind about tofu.

Source of original recipe

Ingredients

For the Crispy Tofu:

  • 400g firm tofu, pressed and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 Tbs cornflour
  • 1 Tbs soy sauce or tamari for gluten-free
  • 1 Tbs sesame oil
  • ½ Tsp garlic powder
  • ½ Tsp white pepper

For the Sesame Sauce:

  • 4 Tbs soy sauce or tamari
  • 2 Tbs rice vinegar
  • 3 Tbs vegetarian hoisin sauce
  • 2 Tbs brown sugar, or maple syrup
  • 2 Tsp sesame oil, toasted
  • 1 Tbs cornflour, for slurry
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 Tsp red chili flakes optional, for heat
  • 4 Tbs water

For Garnish:

  • 1 Tbs sesame seeds toasted
  • 2 spring onions thinly sliced

Instructions

For the Crispy Tofu:

  1. (Skip this step if you're using extra-firm). Press the tofu for 20-30 minutes (ideally overnight) to remove excess moisture.
  2. Break the tofu apart into bite sized pieces by hand to create an interesting texture that will crisp up nicely.
  3. Toss the tofu with soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic powder, and white pepper. Let it marinate for 5–10 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle cornstarch over the tofu and gently toss until evenly coated.

Air-Fry the Tofu:

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 350-400°F (190°C).
  2. Transfer the tofu to a parchment lined air fryer tray or an air fryer bucket.
  3. Air-fry for 12–15 minutes, shaking halfway through, until golden and crispy.

Sesame Sauce:

  1. In a pan over medium low heat, add a tablespoon of oil and allow it to heat up. Sauté the garlic, ginger, and chili flakes for about 30-60 seconds or until fragrant.
  2. Stir in soy sauce, rice vinegar, hoisin sauce, brown sugar, and sesame oil.
  3. Mix the cornstarch slurry (cornstarch + water) and add it to the sauce. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens (about 1–2 minutes).
  4. Add the crispy air fried tofu to the pan and toss until evenly coated in the sauce.

For Garnish:

  1. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds and scallions.
  2. Serve over steamed rice or noodles for a complete meal.
